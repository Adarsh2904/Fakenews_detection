About Fake News Detection

The "Fake News Detection" system is an application designed to identify and classify news articles as either real or fake. The system uses advanced natural language processing techniques and machine learning algorithms to analyze the content of news articles and make accurate predictions about their authenticity.

Misinformation and fake news have become widespread in today's digital age, leading to significant consequences such as public confusion, polarized opinions, and the erosion of trust in media and information sources. The goal of this application is to provide a tool that helps users discern the credibility of news articles they come across online.

The system was built using Python programming language and leverages the scikit-learn library for machine learning tasks. It was trained on a labeled dataset containing examples of both real and fake news articles to learn patterns and features that distinguish between the two types.
